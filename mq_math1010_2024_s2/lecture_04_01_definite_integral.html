2. Regular Partition

Regular Partition

Regular Partition

Regular Partition of [a, b]

  • \(\Delta x = \frac{b-a}{n}\)
  • \(x_i = a + i \Delta x\)
  • \(I_i = [x_{i-1}, x_i]\)

Example

Let \([a, b] = [2, 5]\) and let \(n = 3\)

3. Approximating Sum

Approximating Sums

Min/Max

\begin{align*} m_i &= \min \{f(x) : x \in I_i\} \\ M_i &= \max \{f(x) : x \in I_i\} \end{align*}

Example

  • \(f(x) = x + 4\)
  • \(x \in [2, 5]\)
  • \(n = 3\)

Min/Max Sum

\begin{align*} m_f(n) &= (m_1 + \dots + m_n) \Delta x \\ M_f(n) &= (M_1 + \dots + M_n) \Delta x \end{align*}

Example

  • \(f(x) = x + 4\)
  • \(x \in [2, 5]\)
  • \(n = 3\)

4. Definite Integral

Definite Integral

Definite Integral

For \(f\) continuous, the definite integral is \[ \int_a^b f(x) dx = \lim_{n\to\infty} m_f(n) = \lim_{n\to\infty} M_f(n) \]

Example

\[ \int_0^1 x dx = \frac{1}{2} \]
