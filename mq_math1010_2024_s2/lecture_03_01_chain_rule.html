1. The Chain Rule

The Chain Rule

The Chain Rule

\[ (f \circ g)' (x) = f'(g(x)) g'(x) \]

In \(\tfrac{d}{dx}\) notation if \(y = g(x)\) and \(z = f(y)\), \[ \frac{dz}{dx} = \frac{dz}{dy} \frac{dy}{dx} \]

Example

Calculate the derivative of \(\sin(x^2)\)

Example

Calculate the derivative of \[ \left(\frac{x}{x+1}\right)^2 \]

2. Differentiating Inverse Functions

Differentiating Inverse Functions

Differentiating Inverse Functions

\[ (f^{-1})'(y) = \frac{1}{f'(x)} \text{ where } y = f(x) \]

In \(\tfrac{d}{dx}\) notation, \(\frac{dx}{dy} = \frac{dy}{dx}\)

Example

Let \(g(y) = \sqrt{y}\) for \(y > 0\)

Example

Calculate the derivative of \(g(y) = \ln y\) for \(y > 0\)
